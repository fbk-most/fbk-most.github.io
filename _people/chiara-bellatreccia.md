I am a PhD candidate in the National PhD Program at the University of Pisa in AI & Society, currently based at the University of Bologna, where I am part of the MoST research team. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Physics and a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence, a path that has led me to focus my research on the ethical and fairness-related aspects of AI systems.

My current doctoral research is centered on the development of an optimizer aimed at improving fairness and robustness within the Digital Twin of Bologna.

Outside of academia, I enjoy singing and performing in theatre, as well as playing the piano, which allows me to balance my technical work with creative expression.
