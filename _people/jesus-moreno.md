Jesús Moreno is a PostDoc researcher at the [MoST research unit](/). He currently works on modelling of the Civic Digital Twin.

Before joining [FBK](https://www.fbk.eu/), Jesús was a PhD student at the [Institute for Interdisciplinary Physics and Complex Systems (IFISC)](https://ifisc.uib-csic.es/en/) in Mallorca, focusing his research on agent-based models for the analysis of complexity in social systems. His research interests span from the foundations of complexity to the applications of complexity-centered tools in real-world challenges.
