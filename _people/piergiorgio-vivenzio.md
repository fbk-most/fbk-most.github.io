Piergiorgio Vivenzio is a PhD student in Urban and Regional Development at the Politecnico di Torino.

He was a visiting researcher at FBK's [MoST unit](/) from December 2024 to May 2025. He studied and developed a dynamic simulation model of student housing distribution.

He holds a degree in Statistical and Economic Methods for Decision Making and has worked as a research fellow at the University of Turin. He has also collaborated with the startup MetroPolis SRL.
